Consulting Services

Technical advice goes a long way, and you need experienced technical insight regardless of where your projects sits on the development timeline: evaluating ideas, designing a prototype, building a team or outsourcing to a development shop.

I am a seasoned software engineer and technology entrepreneur. I’ve started and sold consulting companies, developed software products and online applications from the ground up, and managed projects from inception through delivery.

The supply of technical co-founders is low; I’m here to help until you find your long-term solution.

Initial Meeting (Buy me a coffee)

Schedule an hour over to talk about the technical requirements for your idea. I'll answer questions like:

  • Is my idea technically feasible?
  • Which parts of my idea will prove to be a technical challenge?
  • Which parts are already easily solved with existing technology?
  • Is an outsourced team a realistic option for our prototype?
  • Here's my experience so far with my development team - should I be worried?

Research and Follow-up ($150 per hour)

If we agree that you will benefit from our relationship, you are welcome to continue to a further stage. I am generally unavailable for a lengthy engagement, but prefer to schedule in blocks of hours to perform tasks such as:

  • Assess performance of your internal or external technical and project management teams.
  • Systems/Component Integration and test strategies.
  • Reviewing product specifications and anticipating issues before you spend your money.
  • Conduct technical interviews with your team as you look for potential developers or development shops.
  • Proposal reviews prior to submission.
  • Evaluate vendors to help you assess credibility based on their past performance, portfolio review, sample code, etc.
