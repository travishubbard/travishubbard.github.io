Apps

BurpeeBoard - Burpees are great exercise, and you look so awesome doing them. I think we called them a "squat thrust" in the Marine Corps, which I always found a little intimidating - particularly when I was in the front of the line. But I digress...

BurpeeBoard is used to track burpees performed over time. Use the system, get more karma and become way cooler than your friends. Seriously.

Developed specifically for owners of local CrossFit boxes (or similar type establishments) to help build a sense of community, target marketing efforts, organize competitions, etc.

Can be customized to your specifications.

Tech Stack:

  • Meteor
  • Mongo on MongoHQ
  • Amazon Web Services
  • IronWorkers by Iron.io
