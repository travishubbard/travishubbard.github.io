Apps

BurpeeBoard - Burpees are great exercise, I used to do them all day in the Marine Corps...although we just called them a "squat thrust" -- but who am I to split hairs?

BurpeeBoard is used to track burpees performed over time. Use the system, get more karma and become way cool over time. :)

Developed specifically for owners of local CrossFit (or similar) boxes to build a sense of community, marketing, competitions, etc. Can be customized to your specifications.

Tech Stack:

  • Meteor
  • Mongo on MongoHQ
  • Amazon Web Services
  • IronWorkers by Iron.io
