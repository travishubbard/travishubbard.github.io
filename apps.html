Apps

BurpeeBoard - Burpees are great exercise, and you look so awesome doing them. I think we called them a "squat thrust" in the Marine Corps, which I always found a little intimidating - particularly when I was in the front of the line. But I digress...

BurpeeBoard is used to track burpees performed over time. Use the system, get more karma and become way cooler than your friends. Seriously.

BurpeeBoard is a Software as a Service (SaaS) application developed specifically for owners of local CrossFit boxes (or similar type establishments) to help build a sense of community, target marketing efforts, organize competitions, etc.

BurpeeBoard uses Meteor, stores its data on MongoHQ and is hosted on AWS. We use IronWorkers via Iron.io to demonstrate the "Full Stack Reactivity" principle of Meteor apps.
